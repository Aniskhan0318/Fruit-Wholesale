About Us

Passion for Fresh Produce, Built on Trust

Inayat Veggies is an independent wholesale trader with over a decade of hands-on experience sourcing and supplying fresh fruit and vegetables. Working directly with growers and established suppliers, the business delivers quality produce to a wide range of customers — from local market stalls to restaurants and independent retailers.

Every order is personally handled with attention to freshness, fair pricing, and reliable delivery. This is a business built on relationships — with growers who share a commitment to quality, and with customers who rely on consistency.

The sourcing operation runs 7 days a week, starting at the early market hours to select only the best available stock. Customers receive produce that is inspected and packed the same day it is ordered.

📍 Operating from Hyderabad — supplying customers across the surrounding area since 1990s.
